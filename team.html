The Team

Dr. Srinivasan

Dr. Aravind Srinivasan is a professor with the Department of Computer Science and the Institute for Advanced Computer Studies. He has experience in many research areas including algorithms and combinatorial optimization, probabilistic methods, algorithmic statistics, and machine learning, network science, algorithms, and technology for public health, algorithms and machine learning in (cancer) genomics, algorithms in networking (wireless and peer-to-peer), social networks, and distributed computing, AI and algorithms in E-commerce, and computational approaches to sustainable growth.

Ms. Inge

Ms. Igne leads information literacy instruction for first year and general education courses. She loves working with undergraduate students for anything related to research, using the libraries, or just adjusting to academic life on campus. She would also be happy to speak with instructors of first year/general education courses about how the Libraries can support their students. Her research interests include critical pedagogy, information literacy, student development, and the history of cultural institutions.
